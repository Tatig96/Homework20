Introduction

Cue Planner Mobile Application The Cue planner app is a fun and innovative way to keep up with daily reminders. Imagine a fusion of Google and Apple calendar apps... Minimalism, creativity, innovation, and functionality.

PCOS Challenge Charity Redesign The PCOS Challege is a charity concerning polycystic ovarian syndrome in women. Their website and branding was extremely outdated, taking away from the significance of the cause. Our team took great pride in updating the web-design, while staying true to the PCOS branding and color usage/gradient.
